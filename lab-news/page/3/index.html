New publication describing archaea in freshwater wetlands

Our publication describing the unexplored archaeal diversity in the Old Woman Creek freshwater wetland has been accepted to Environmental Microbiology. High-resolution sequencing reveals unexplored archaeal diversity in freshwater wetland soils This research, which was led by Adrienne, results from work done with our great collaborators in the Wrighton Lab.
