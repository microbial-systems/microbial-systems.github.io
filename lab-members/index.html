Graduate Students

Alex Romero

Alex is a MS student in Integrative Biology working on the Dog Gut Microbiome project. Alex is also interested in understanding how incorporating real research experiences into first-year Biology labs affects learning outcomes.

Derick Singleton

Derick is a MS student in Integrative Biology studying antimicrobial resistance with multi-omics data from freshwater wetlands soils.

Lucia Guatney

Lucia is a PhD student from the Computational Bioscience Program. Lucia’s research aims to improve metagenomic protein annotations with the end goal of better predicting metabolites produced from microbial communities. In part, Lucia hopes to incorporate and then validate non-homology methods into present annotation tools and methods.
